Pizza Dough Recipe (for a 32 cm pizza)

Instructions:

  1. Combine ingredients

    Add all the ingredients at once: semolina, flour, water, olive oil, salt, and yeast.

  2. Knead the dough

    Knead by hand or with a stand mixer for about 20 minutes until smooth and elastic.

  3. First rise (warm spot)

    Let the dough rest in a warm spot for 2 hours to activate the yeast.

  4. Cold fermentation (fridge)

    Place the dough in the refrigerator for 24 hours for better flavor.

  5. Bring to room temperature

    Let the dough rest at room temperature for 2 hours before baking.

  6. Stretch and bake

    Stretch into a 32 cm pizza base, top as desired, and bake.
