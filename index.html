Pizza Dough Recipe (for a 32 cm pizza)

Instructions:

  1. Combine ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, add all the ingredients at once: semolina, flour, water, olive oil, salt, and yeast.
  2. Knead the dough: Knead the mixture by hand (or with a stand mixer) for about 20 minutes, until the dough is smooth, elastic, and no longer sticky.
  3. First rise (warm spot): Cover the dough with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let it rest in a warm spot for 2 hours. This allows the yeast to start fermenting and developing flavor.
  4. Cold fermentation (fridge): After the initial rise, place the dough in the refrigerator for 24 hours. This slow fermentation improves flavor and texture.
  5. Bring to room temperature and shape: Two hours before baking, take the dough out of the fridge. Shape it into a pizza ball (300 g) and let it rest at room temperature for 2 hours.
